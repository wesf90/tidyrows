How to Use TidyRows: Format Spreadsheet Data Easily

TidyRows is a simple, free tool designed to help you format raw spreadsheet data into a clean, readable structure. Whether you're preparing client data for reports, organizing information for Google Docs, ClickUp, or any other platform, TidyRows makes the process quick and effortless.

What is TidyRows For?

TidyRows is perfect for anyone who needs to transform tab-separated data (like from Google Sheets or Excel) into a structured format. It’s ideal for:

  • Freelancers and small business owners organizing client information.
  • Marketers preparing data for reports or presentations.
  • Anyone needing to format data for WYSIWYG editors like Google Docs or Notion.

How to Use TidyRows in 3 Simple Steps

  1. Paste Your Data: Copy your tab-separated data from a spreadsheet (e.g., Google Sheets) and paste it anywhere on the page using Ctrl+V (or Cmd+V on Mac).
  2. Customize Formatting: Adjust the options in the top menu:
    • Separator: Choose the separator between keys and values (e.g., ": ").
    • Same Line?: Toggle whether keys and values appear on the same line or separate lines.
    • Rows to Format: Select whether to format the last row only or all rows.
  3. Copy the Result: Use the buttons to copy your formatted data in your preferred format:
    • Plain Text: Simple text with newlines.
    • Markdown: Bold keys in Markdown syntax.
    • HTML: Formatted with HTML tags for rich text editors.
    • Formatted: Copy with bold keys and line breaks for WYSIWYG editors like Google Docs.

Why Choose TidyRows?

TidyRows is a lightweight, no-installation tool that runs directly in your browser. It’s perfect for quickly formatting spreadsheet data without complex software. Whether you’re a beginner or a pro, TidyRows saves you time and ensures your data looks professional.
